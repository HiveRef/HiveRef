## Reporting a Vulnerability

Please report suspected vulnerabilities **privately** by emailing
**security@example.com**. Do **not** open public GitHub issues for security
reports.

Include the following details in your report (as available):
- Affected version(s) and environment
- Clear reproduction steps or proof of concept
- Expected vs. actual behavior
- Impact assessment (confidentiality, integrity, availability)
- Any suggested remediation

What to expect after you report:
- **Acknowledgement:** within 3 business days
- **Initial triage update:** within 7 business days
- **Status updates:** at least every 14 days until resolution

We follow a coordinated disclosure process: we validate the report, develop and
test a fix, and then publish a security advisory with remediation guidance once
users have had a reasonable opportunity to update. If a report is out of scope
or cannot be reproduced, we will explain the decision and next steps.
